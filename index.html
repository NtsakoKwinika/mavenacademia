Maven Academia

Maven Academia is an education-focused tutoring company that provides high-quality Mathematics and Physical Science support across all curricula, with a strong emphasis on academic excellence, conceptual understanding, and learner confidence

What we do as a tutoring company Offer specialised tutoring in Mathematics and Physical Science We support learners from different education systems (CAPS, IEB, Cambridge, and other curricula), with a strong focus on the FET phase while remaining flexible to support other grades where needed. Provide structured, results-driven academic support, our tutoring is aligned to school syllabi and exam requirements, helping learners improve marks, master difficult concepts, and develop strong problem-solving skills. Bridge learning gaps and strengthen foundations. We identify learner weaknesses early and provide targeted intervention to close gaps in mathematical and scientific understanding. Prepare learners for assessments and examinations, we assist with class tests, assignments, practical understanding, exams, and standardized assessments such as NBT preparation, ensuring learners are exam-ready and confident. Use blended and digital learning platforms Maven Academia integrates Microsoft Teams and Google Classroom to deliver online lessons, share resources, track progress, and ensure continuity of learning beyond physical sessions. Our mission is to make Mathematics and Science more accessible, especially for learners who may not be receiving sufficient support at school, and to encourage progression into science, engineering, and technology careers.
